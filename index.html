Common questions

Helpful answers for families and referral partners.

How do referral sources send documents?

Referral sources may use the secure online referral form or send referral documents by secure fax to (763) 762-6132. They may also contact Evergreen by email at marie@evergreenhomehcs.com or evergreenhomehcs@gmail.com.

How quickly can Evergreen review a placement?

Evergreen works quickly with families, case managers, social workers, and discharge planners to determine fit, schedule tours, and complete assessment steps.

What type of setting is Evergreen?

Evergreen is a small 5-bed assisted living home in Coon Rapids, Minnesota, designed for residents who benefit from a calm home-like setting and consistent support.

Which payer sources are listed for review?

Evergreen accepts private pay, long-term care insurance, CADI, BI, and EW supportive referrals when the resident’s needs and placement fit are appropriate.
